About me

I am a fifth year PhD Candidate in Aerospace Engineering at Georgia Tech, advised by Prof. Kyriakos G. Vamvoudakis. I obtained my Diploma (joint BSc and MSc degree) in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki in 2018, where I worked with Prof. George A. Rovithakis. I also hold MSc Degrees in Aerospace Engineering and Mathematics, both obtained from Georgia Tech, in 2022 and 2023, respectively.
